A long, long time ago an old woman and an old man lived a quiet life in a small cottage by the river. One day the woman decided to make a gingerbread man for them to eat. 
The woman put ginger bread man in the oven to bake.
Soon enough, he was ready and the woman opened the oven door, but to her surprise the Gingerbread man jumped out of it. 
“Don’t eat me!" he shouted in terror and ran out of the door.
